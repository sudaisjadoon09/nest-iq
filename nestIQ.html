AI Engine Architecture
NESTIQ's AI stack processes 50+ data signals per listing. The NLP layer understands lifestyle descriptions, not just specs. Computer Vision analyses window orientation, room quality, and photo authenticity simultaneously.
USER QUERY (Natural Language) ↓ [NLP Parser · BERT-Large] INTENT EXTRACTION ├─ Budget Range ├─ Location + Radius ├─ Lifestyle Tags └─ Implicit Preferences ↓ [Matching Engine · XGBoost] LISTING SCORE (0-100%) ├─ Budget Fit (35%) ├─ Location Score (25%) ├─ Feature Match (25%) └─ Agent Trust Score (15%) ↓ [Ranking + Output] TOP-5 AI-RANKED RESULTS

Blockchain Trust Layer
Every verified property title is hashed and stored on Ethereum L2 (Polygon). Cross-referenced with national land registries in 12 countries. The verification is cryptographically permanent — impossible to forge, alter, or delete.
  • 1
    Document Upload — Agent submits title deeds, ownership proof
  • 2
    Registry Cross-Check — AI verifies against national land database
  • 3
    Cryptographic Hash — Unique fingerprint stored on blockchain forever
  • 4
    ⛓ Badge Awarded — Mathematically impossible to fake
Stack: Ethereum L2 (Polygon) · IPFS · ZK-SNARK Proofs
